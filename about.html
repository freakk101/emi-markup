Strong Relationships = Health and Happiness

Live happier and healthier with Emi

Emi means “smile” in Japanese

Emi was born out of life experience. We all want our relationship with our partner to be great, but maintaining a happy and healthy relationship sounds like a lot of work. We want to make this simple and easy for you, by bringing simple mindful practices at your fingertips. We are constantly fighting unrealistic idealistic conceptions of marriage and romantic relationships. At Emi, we want to make relationship fitness more mainstream and show every couple that meaningful relationships are diverse as the people that are a part of them. So come join our community, and build happy healthy relationship habits with us.
